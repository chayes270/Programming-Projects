This is a Space Invaders/Galaga style game where the player
must shoot at aliens in order to win.

To play this game, open group_10_assignment7.pde in Processing
and click play.

Controls:
    "A"/"a"/"Left Arrow" to move left
    "D"/"d"/"Right Arrow" to move right
    "Spacebar" to shoot
    Hover over a barrier with the mouse and press "r" to repair it. Only usable once per barrier.


Rules of Play:
  The player must kill all aliens to win.

  If the aliens reach the end of the screen or lower the
  player's HP to zero, game over!
